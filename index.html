Art Supplies for Children

Help us raise funds to supply school-aged kids with art supplies.

At Art Supplies for Children, we are committed to bringing creative values to the kids of today. Learning creative techniques at a young age can ensure the child gets a hold of important lessons and experiences such as finishing a project on your own, understanding making mistakes and how to fix them, and finally patience when it comes to completing slow-paced tasks.
The experience of understanding art is vital for the growth of the youth, and ASC is committed to providing art supplies of several different media to any family that signs up.
